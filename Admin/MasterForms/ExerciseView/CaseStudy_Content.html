Your Role

As the regional after-sales service head at NextGen Motors, Shashi, you are tasked with aligning after-sales services with the CEO's strategy for customized customer services. Together with other regional heads, you need to categorize focus areas into 3-year and 5-year plans respectively.    

Your Task

The leadership has asked you as the regional service manager, to prioritize four areas for your region for the first 3 years and the remaining for the first 5 years.

While prioritizing, communicate the following clearly along with rationale for the same:

  • List the four areas of focus for 3 and 5-year strategies respectively.
  • List the factors considered while creating 3 and 5-year strategies respectively.
  • Communicate clearly, any assumptions made while developing the strategy and the plan of action.

Areas of focus

Focus Area

What is it?

Leadership Rationale

Predictive Analytics

-    Predict component failure with data analytics.

-    Optimize maintenance schedules.

-    Will help in minimizing repair costs.

-    Will improve vehicle reliability.

-    Will improve customer experience.

Five years ago, NextGen Motors equipped all models with IoT sensors, now generating data from 1 million vehicles.

 

The leadership plans to use this data for predictive maintenance and performance monitoring.

Enhance EV Service Capabilities

-    Specialized training for EV service technicians

-    Bring state of the art EV servicing capabilities

NextGen Motors stands as a frontrunner in the country's 2-wheeler EV sector.

 

The rapid evolution within the EV landscape necessitates continuous learning and upskilling of the workforce.

 

NextGen Motors aims to establish state-of-the-art EV service capabilities in all its service centres across the country.

 

Mobile Service Units

-    Introduction of mobile service units

-    Equipped with tools and diagnostic equipment to provide on-site repairs and maintenance.

-    Enhances convenience for customers and expands service reach to remote areas.

 

NextGen Motors aims to enhance its service offerings with new mobile units to improve customer experience, providing quick support during emergencies and convenient maintenance for those preferring not to visit service centres.

 

These units are expected to appeal to customers over 40 years of age, positioning NextGen Motors to compete effectively in this market.

 

AR and VR

-    Virtual inspection assistance for technicians

-    Interactive training and repair guides

-    Enables technicians to visualize complex repairs and access real-time support from experts.

Recent strides in Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) have birthed new contenders in the after-sales service arena. NextGen Motors has already leveraged AR and VR for virtual showrooms and intends to deploy this technology for service provisions too.

 

However, doing so poses unique challenges that include technician training and seamless integration with existing systems.

 

Green Initiative

-    Implementation of eco-friendly practices and technologies in service operations

NextGen Motors, known for its strong commitment to sustainability, boasts one of the highest ESG ratings in the country, positively impacting its market value and access to government incentives.

 

Following a thorough internal audit, the company identified energy efficiency improvements in its after-sales service division and is dedicated to adopting eco-friendly practices in this area.

 

Blockchain for Supply Chain Management

-    Blockchain technology for transparent and secure supply chain

NextGen Motors is facing a rise in vehicle breakdowns due to counterfeit spare parts, as seen by more calls to their customer support.

 

The company's leadership is pushing for greater supply chain transparency to tackle this issue.

 

Collaborative Partnerships

-    Strategic partnerships with technology companies, startups, and other industry players to leverage expertise, access new technologies, and co-create innovative solutions for automotive service.

NextGen Motors' in-house developed app allows customers to manage vehicle health and services.

 

Due to increased usability complaints, the company plans to collaborate with tech partners for app refinement and maintenance.

 

Customer Experience Enhancement

-    Introduction of personalized service experiences, such as concierge services, vehicle customization options, and loyalty programs.

NextGen Motors is committed to personalization in its products and services.

 

However, the company has seen an increase in the number of competitors in the market.

 

Leadership has directed the after-sales team to thoroughly review and improve the customer experience with the introduction of more personalized after sales services.

Market Competitors in your region

Given below are the details of major competitors who are active in your region:

  1. AutoServeX: Launched in 2022, this app-based vehicle service provider quickly became a unicorn, offering doorstep services that resonated with users aged 18-35. Praised for its user-friendly interface and convenience, it has swiftly gained popularity in the market.

Despite losing customers, NextGen Motors maintains its reputation for quality of service. A recent report highlighted dissatisfaction among AutoServeX customers due to the use of counterfeit spare parts.

  1. Athena Electric: Athena Electric, a Bangalore based EV manufacturer of 2 wheelers, innovates sales and service with AR and VR headsets. Technicians use them for efficient maintenance, improving service quality through data insights. This startup has quickly developed a loyal customer base in tier 1 cities over the past few years.
  1. Seoul Auto: Seoul Auto, a leading Korean 2-wheeler manufacturer, offers an extensive network of nationwide service centres. While known for affordable vehicles and budget-friendly after-sales service, their customer satisfaction lags due to subpar service quality.
  1. Nakamura Motors: Nakamura Motors is a Japanese 2-wheeler manufacturer renowned for their high-quality manufacturing of vehicles and spare parts. They have a few service centres located across Tier 1 and select tier 2 cities of the country.
  1. Rhine Auto: Rhine Auto, a German vehicle manufacturer, is celebrated for its top-notch products and durable spare parts. However, their pricey after-sales customer service has led to a decline in market share over time.

You are playing the role of Ramesh Chinnadurai.

Ramesh joined MRDL two months back as Director for the Basic Research Division. Ramesh’s early career was promising. He has a B.Tech in Electronics from NIT, Calicut, where he was viewed as one among the technical elites. He also has a master’s degree in Information Technology from International Institute of Information Technology, Bangalore. He started his career as a Research Engineer, with Siemens Corporate Technology. He, then moved to Robert Bosch Engineering and Research Solutions Germany, where he was involved in Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) ECU projects for customers from BRICK countries. He progressed to setting up Center of Competence in Automotive Transmission Control SW for CVT, AMT & AT systems in Bangalore. He was handpicked by Sanjeev Ghani the Managing Director of MRDL for his current role

You need to analyse the case and present your recommendation to Sanjeev Ghani – Managing Director of MRDL.

Your analysis would include the following in the form of a presentation:

  1. A clear recommendation of three products
  2. Point of view on why you recommend this product in terms of technology available, market consideration and competition
  3. Point of view on disruptive trends noticed in terms of technology available, market consideration and competition which stopped you from recommending the remaining four products

If you feel the need to make additional assumptions to carry out this task, you are free to do so, provided you are able to give an adequate explanation for the assumptions made.
